Hello! I’m Bhanu G.

A photographer driven by the desire to tell stories through my lens.

With a keen eye for detail and a passion for capturing genuine emotions, I strive to create timeless images that resonate deeply.

My journey into photography began by capturing portraits of friends and the natural beauty around my hometown. Over time, what started as a personal interest turned into a deep passion. Even after facing setbacks like losing my first camera I never gave up. I kept learning, creating, and pushing forward with what I had.

I specialize in portraits, nature, and event moments always aiming to bring out the unique beauty in every subject I capture.
